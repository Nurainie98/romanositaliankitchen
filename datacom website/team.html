About Us

Romano's Italian Kitchen was created in 2011 out of a passion for amazing food and service. Then one-woman operation saw rapid growth after opening its very first restaurant in Kuala Lumpur. Through word of mouth, a lot of hard work and a commitment to producing an authentic restaurant, Romano's Italian Kitchen has become one of the most beloved restaurant happening in Kuala Lumpur with the best imported chef, Monosilio.


We believe that using the freshest ingredients produces the best results. We are tireless in staying abreast of the latest food trends. And we are obsessed with creating customized menus and culinary masterpieces for our beloved customers.Simply put: we exist to make your vision a reality
